About Map the Paths

In 2019 I bought my first 360 camera, a GoPro Fusion. I used the camera to start recording some of my hikes to share with friends, family and YouTube.

A friend then told me this content could be uploaded and mapped on Google Street View. So I started uploading my content to Google.

Many millions of steps later I had lots of content on Street View but no easy way to search and share it.

I wanted to be able to quickly pull up images taken by bike. Or at the top of a summit. Unfortunately this meant knowing where to look and dropping a peg man onto Google Maps.

And so the idea Map the Paths was born.

Map the Paths is a platform for uploading and managing off-road street-level imagery captured by anyone, anywhere in the world.

Map the Paths is an open-source project. You can view the source code here. Pull request are always welcomed.

About Trek View

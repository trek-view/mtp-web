Getting Started

We've created a quick start guide with everything you need to start capturing photos to create your own sequences.

      Get a camera: You can use any 360 camera. We've created our own Trek Pack's for hiking, cycling and swimming you can use as inspiration.
      Start shooting: This is the fun part! Here are some of recommended camera / kit settings for your shoot.
      Upload your images: Our Uploader allows you to easily upload your outdoor tour to multiple platforms, including Map the Paths.
      Manage your sequences: Group your sequences into Tours, or guide viewers through your adventure using Guidebooks.
      Share your adventures: Other explorers are able to discover all the content you've uploaded and created.

What are you waiting for? Get mapping!
